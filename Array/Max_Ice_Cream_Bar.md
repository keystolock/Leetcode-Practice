# LeetCode Problem: Maximum Ice Cream Bars

## Problem Statement
You are given an array `costs` where `costs[i]` is the cost of the `i-th` ice cream bar.  
You have `coins` coins to spend, and you want to buy as many ice cream bars as possible.  

Return the **maximum number of ice cream bars** you can buy with the given coins.

---

## Example
**Input:**
costs = [1,3,2,4,1], coins = 7

**Output:**
4

**Explanation:**
You can buy ice creams with costs `[1,2,1,3]` for a total of 7 coins.

---

## Constraints
- \(1 \leq costs.length \leq 10^5\)  
- \(1 \leq costs[i] \leq 10^5\)  
- \(1 \leq coins \leq 10^8\)
